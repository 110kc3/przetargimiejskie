# Spike — Puławy (Lubelskie · powiat puławski)

> **Status:** spike LIVE-VERIFIED — 2026-06-27. VERDICT: NO-BUILD (Low effort to confirm, but nothing to build).

## TL;DR

Gmina Miasto Puławy (pop. ~47 k) sells municipal flats **exclusively bezprzetargowo** — directly to sitting tenants under art. 34 ustawy o gospodarce nieruchomościami, no open auction. Public auctions (*przetarg ustny nieograniczony*) exist only for undeveloped land and commercial parcels. The housing manager "Nieruchomości Puławskie" holds tenders only for market-hall stall rentals. No achieved-price stream from flat auctions exists because no flat auctions are held.

---

## 1. Sells municipal property at auction?

**Municipal flats: NO open auction. Confirmed bezprzetargowa only.**

Live-verified from the official city portal: the April 2022 announcement (tagged `#Sprzedaż lokali mieszkalnych`) states explicitly:

> "Prezydent Miasta Puławy informuje, że [...] lokale mieszkalne przeznaczone zostały do sprzedaży **w drodze bezprzetargowej** na rzecz najemców"
> — ul. Krańcowa 11/4 and ul. Polna 1A/77 offered to sitting tenants under art. 34 ust. 1 pkt 1 and 2 UGN.

Source: https://www.pulawy.eu/pl/tablica-ogloszen/informacja-dotyczaca-wywieszenia-wykazu-nieruchomosci--sprzedaz-2-lokali-mieszkalnych.html

Public auctions (*przetarg ustny nieograniczony*) are held, but only for **niezabudowane nieruchomości** (undeveloped land). The Aug 2024 sample auction was for a plot at ul. Towarowa — explicitly described as "niezabudowanej nieruchomości", with auction date 2025-10-25.

Source: https://www.pulawy.eu/pl/nieruchomosci/przetargi-z-zakresu-nieruchomosci/ogloszenie-o-przetargu-ustnym-nieograniczonym-na-sprzedaz-niezabudowanej-nieruchomosci-przy-ul-towarowej-w-pulawach.html

No *ustny przetarg nieograniczony na sprzedaż lokali mieszkalnych* found in any search or live page, consistent across multiple query angles.

## 3. Format + rendering

- **pulawy.eu** — static HTML pages generated by SmartSite (BIT Sp. z o.o.) CMS. Auction announcements are individual article pages with a PDF attachment containing full details. Rendered cleanly via web_fetch; no SPA, no auth, no bot block observed.
- **umpulawy.bip.lubelskie.pl** — standard Lubelskie-province BIP platform (PHP, server-side rendered). Large pages (>60 k chars); content is HTML, no JS required. No auth.
- **np.pulawy.pl** — WordPress 6.5. Clean HTML. Przetargi category page renders fully. No bot block.
- **PDF attachments** — attached to each announcement as separate files (e.g., `Ogłoszenie_przetarg_Towarowa.pdf`, 104 KB). These are text PDFs (not scanned), machine-readable. However, since flat auctions don't occur, this is moot for the aggregator use case.

## 4. Volume + achieved-price stream

- **Flat auctions:** 0 confirmed — city policy is 100% bezprzetargowa to tenants.
- **Land/commercial auctions:** occasional, low frequency. One live example found (Oct 2024 land plot). No dedicated results page on pulawy.eu; no wyniki przetargów section found in either BIP or portal.
- **Nieruchomości Puławskie auctions:** commercial stall rentals only (Hala Targowa). No residential. No results publication found.
- **Achieved-price stream:** does not exist for flats; for land auctions it would need to be scraped from BIP or individual page PDFs — no structured feed.

## 5. Adapter effort + verdict

**Closest analog among known cities:** None — Puławy is the pure bezprzetargowa case (flats go directly to tenants), unlike Gliwice/ZGM which runs open flat auctions. The nearest analog in terms of *what not to build* is Tarnowskie Góry (land-only auctions) but even there land auctions exist; here land/commercial auctions exist but are not the aggregator's target.

**Blockers:**
1. No *ustny przetarg nieograniczony na sprzedaż lokali mieszkalnych* exists — the primary aggregator signal is absent.
2. Flat sales go directly from wykaz to tenant, bypassing the auction step entirely. There is no bidding, no achieved price, nothing to aggregate.

**Risks:** None from a build perspective — the risk is the sunk cost of investigation, which this spike resolves.

**VERDICT: NO-BUILD** — Puławy does not hold open flat auctions. Municipal flats are sold exclusively bezprzetargowo to sitting tenants. The city's auction activity (land plots, occasional commercial) is out of scope for the aggregator. No adapter warranted.

**Confidence: High** — confirmed via live fetch of two separate official pages plus multiple search corroborations.
